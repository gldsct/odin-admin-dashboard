Fights

Luck v/s Lotus

Luck Voltia v/s Lotus Whomalt is a fight that occurs during an exploration of a newly emerged dungeon. It resulted in a win for Luck, after his friends - Asta and Noelle - helped him, however, Lotus managed to escape.

Luck v/s Priest

Asta, Magna Swing, Luck Voltia, and Gauche Adlai vs. Four Priests is a fight that occurs in the maze within the Underwater Temple. It resulted in an easy victory for Luck and his friends.

Luck v/s Vetto

Magna Swing and Luck Voltia vs. Vetto is a fight that occurs in the maze within the Underwater Temple. Although Luck and Magna try their best, Vetto easily manages to defeat them.

Luck v/s Team L

Luck (Team K) v/s Team L is the sixth fight of the Royal Knights Selection Exam. Although his "Thunderclap Crumbling Orbs" are ineffective in destroying the crystal, he eventually destroys it by kicking his opponent into the crystal.

Luck v/s Team I

Luck (Team K) v/s Team I is the twelfth fight of the Royal Knights Selection Exam. Luck's team combines their magic to create a steel railgun that shoots a Thunder Arrow, but their attempt is thwarted by Rill throwing the arrow back at them, destroying their crystal.

Luck v/s Reve

Vanessa Enoteca, Magna Swing, Luck Voltia, Charmy Pappitson, and Sally v/s Reve is a fight that occurs in the Glamour World. The Black Bulls and Sally are pulled into Reve's "Glamour World," where the odds are highly against them. The tide is turned when Luck thinks up an idea to summon Captain Dorothy Unsworth, who battles Reve until Glamour World collapses. The fight ends with the Black Bulls victorious.

Magic

Lightning Magic
Luck uses this magic attribute to manipulate lightning. He concentrates the lightning on his hand before releasing it towards his opponent.
True Lightning Magic
Luck uses the Heart Kingdom's magic technique and natural mana to generate and manipulate real lightning.
Creation Magic
Luck uses this form of magic to shape objects from lightning. He clads himself with lightning to enhance his fighting skills.
